Frequently asked questions

Before starting SEO.

How long does SEO positioning take?

It depends on competition, the state of your website and current authority. SEO usually works in monthly cycles because Google needs consistent signals.

Does SEO replace paid ads?

Not necessarily. SEO builds organic visibility and paid ads accelerate demand. They can work together when a business needs both short-term and long-term results.

Do I need a website?

It helps a lot. If you do not yet have a clear website, we can start with Google Business Profile and then create or improve key pages.

Why connect WhatsApp?

Because many customers do not want to fill out forms. WhatsApp reduces friction, allows fast responses and makes it easier to close when intent is strong.

Are results guaranteed?

We do not promise exact rankings. We work with strategy, execution and measurement to improve visibility, traffic and commercial opportunities sustainably.
