What happens next

Three phases from first conversation to ongoing growth.

Each step has a clear owner. We guide the strategy and execution while you provide the business context, approvals and feedback needed to move quickly.

Phase 01

Start

  1. 01

    Demo Meeting

    We understand your business, goals, current online presence and growth opportunities.

    Share your priorities, market and current challenges.

  2. 02

    Letter of Intent Signed

    We confirm the partnership, align on expectations and define the next steps.

    Confirm scope, decision makers and timing.

  3. 03

    First Payment Completed

    The project officially begins and our team starts working on your SEO positioning plan.

    Complete payment so onboarding can begin without delay.

Phase 02

Build

  1. 04

    Strategy Brief Meeting

    We build your full marketing brief and company profile around brand, services, audience, competitors, website status, local market and priorities.

    Bring access, context, offers, service areas and business goals.

  2. 05

    SEO Foundations

    We prepare the foundations needed to position your business online, including structure, keywords, competitors, Google Business Profile, local SEO and content direction.

    Provide missing account access, brand details and any existing assets.

  3. 06

    First Delivery

    We present the first version of your key digital assets, such as website structure, landing pages or Google Business Profile improvements.

    Review the first delivery against your business reality.

  4. 07

    Review & Adjustments

    You review the first delivery, share feedback and we make the necessary adjustments.

    Give consolidated feedback so revisions stay focused.

Phase 03

Grow

  1. 08

    Launch & Tracking

    Once approved, we launch or update the agreed assets and begin tracking performance.

    Approve launch and confirm any final business details.

  2. 09

    Monthly Growth Meeting

    We meet every month to review SEO progress, rankings, traffic, leads and next actions.

    Share lead quality, sales feedback and business priorities.

  3. 10

    Continuous Optimization

    We keep improving your visibility, online presence and SEO positioning month after month.

    Keep us informed as offers, locations or goals change.
