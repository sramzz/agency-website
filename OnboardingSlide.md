# Your SEO Onboarding Journey

A clear step-by-step view of what happens after you invest and how our partnership begins.

---

## 01. 🤝 Demo Meeting  
We understand your business, goals, current online presence, and growth opportunities.

---

## 02. 📝 Letter of Intent Signed  
We confirm the partnership, align on expectations, and define the next steps.

---

## 03. 💳 First Payment Completed  
The project officially begins, and our team starts working on your SEO positioning plan.

---

## 04. 🧭 Strategy Brief Meeting  
We meet with you to build your full marketing brief and company profile.

**We review:**  
- 🏷️ Brand identity  
- 🛍️ Products or services  
- 🎯 Target audience  
- 🏆 Main competitors  
- 🌐 Website/domain status  
- 📍 Local market positioning  
- 📈 Business goals and priorities  

---

## 05. 🔎 SEO Foundations  
We prepare the foundations needed to position your business online.

**This may include:**  
- 🧱 Website structure and SEO recommendations  
- 🔑 Keyword and competitor research  
- 📍 Google Business Profile setup or optimization  
- 🗺️ Local SEO foundations  
- ✍️ Content and positioning direction  

---

## 06. 🚀 First Delivery  
We present the first version of your key digital assets, such as your website structure, landing pages, or Google Business Profile improvements.

---

## 07. 🔁 Review & Adjustments  
You review the first delivery, share feedback, and we make the necessary adjustments.

---

## 08. 🌍 Launch & Tracking  
Once approved, we launch or update the agreed assets and begin tracking performance.

---

## 09. 📊 Monthly Growth Meeting  
We meet every month to review SEO progress, rankings, traffic, leads, and next actions.

---

## 10. 📈 Continuous Optimization  
We keep improving your visibility, online presence, and SEO positioning month after month.
